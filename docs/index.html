to start of we will learn how to turn numbers to number word in arabic.

turning 123 to arabic 123


first is start to seperate them we take 123 to 100, 20, 3.
then we take the hundreds place which is 100, 100 in arabic is "mi'a".
next is take the ones place which is 3, 3 in arabic is "thalath".
lastly is 20 which is "ishrun", then we combine them.
to combine them is the hundreds place the ones then the tens, to seperate them use "wa"
our result should be:

mi'a wa thalathah wa ishrun

the thousands place

lets step up a challenge and go for the thousands!
lets use 123456 as our number like turning 123 to arabic 123 we will do the same for 456
now we should be left with "mi'a wa thalathah wa ishrun" and "arba'un wa sitta wa hhamsun"
to combine them two we need to add "alaaf" to the thousands place. and combine them with "wa"
and our result should be:

mi'a wa thalathah wa ishrun alaaf wa arba'un wa sitta wa hhamsun

this technique only works to a certain extent like number 12 if we use this technique we would have "ashar wa ithnan" but its actually "ithna ashar"
